***Aczone (dapsone topical) can cause side effects/reactions in a patient with Acne. These side effects include hives; difficult breathing; swelling of your face, lips, tongue, or throat. Call your doctor at once if you have: severe sore throat; unusual thoughts or behavior; a red blood cell disorder - back pain, pale or yellowed skin, dark colored urine, fever, confusion or weakness; pancreas problems - severe pain in your upper stomach spreading to your back, nausea and vomiting, fast heart rate; or severe skin reaction - fever, sore throat, swelling in your face or tongue, burning in your eyes, skin pain followed by a red or purple skin rash that spreads (especially in the face or upper body) and causes blistering and peeling.
